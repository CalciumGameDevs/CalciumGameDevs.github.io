Zombie Tag Privacy Policy


Important notice.

We can only ensure the privacy of your data when using an official game client, therefore, we strongly recomend that you do not use an unofficial modded client.


What do we use data for?

We use your data to authenticate you in order to provide access to online multiplayer in Zombie Tag.


What data do we use?

The table below shows what data we use and what we use it for:

Data Use
Epic Account ID Your epic account ID is used by the Zombie Tag client to fetch your profile information.
Username Your username is used by the Zombie Tag client in online multiplayer matches to allow other players to identify you and other players in the match.
Friends list Your friends list is used by the Zombie Tag client to display your friends in game and make it easy for you to invite a friend to an online multiplayer match.
Friend nicknames Friend nicknames are used by the Zombie Tag client to display them in game instead of that user's default username. These nicknames will be kept strictly on your local game client and will not be shared with other players or Calcium Games.
Online status Your online status is modified to show other players that you are in the game and will also display whether you are in a match or on the menu.
Linked account display names The linked account name of the platform that the game client is launched from may be used instead of your Epic account username if the platform requires this.
Language preference Your language preference may be collected by the game client in order to display the game in your prefered language, however it is not garenteed that your prefered language will be available.

Who do we share data with?

Your Epic Account ID (and therefore your profile information such as username) will be shared with the game clients of players in the same online match as you in order to display your username and profile. We will never share any other data with anyone except Epic Games where required.
